Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a prompt?
Each message you send to AnalystBot counts as 1 prompt. Follow-up questions count too.
What data sources can I connect?
Starter: Google Sheets (shareable link) or CSV/Excel file upload. Growth and Pro add more sources over time.
Can I change plans?
Yes, upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect at the next billing cycle.
Is my data private?
Yes. Each customer's data is stored in an isolated folder. We never share or use your data.
How do I pay?
Payments are processed securely via Stripe. You can pay with any major credit or debit card.
